Simplifies a SPolygon by reducing the number of edges.

The simplified shape will either be a subset or a superset of the original shape, depending on the ShapeModifyMode. The procedure sequentially eliminates edges until either the change in area (ratio) exceeds max_area_delta or the number of edges < 4.
